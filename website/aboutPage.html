  Hello, Welcome to the about page!

  This website allows a user to look a stock's
  current value and see how relative information
  and events affected that stock. 

  Relative information is anything outside of 
  the company's day to day business transactions. 
  For example news and social media.
    
  We believe that major events outside of company 
  deals can heavily affect their stock, with this
  website we can show it to be true or false.
